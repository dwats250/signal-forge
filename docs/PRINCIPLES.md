# Principles

## Trading Philosophy

- Nonlinearity over linear prediction
- Volatility creates opportunity windows
- Path dependency matters
- Escape windows exist even in wrong trades
- Avoid binary thinking
- Favor asymmetric payoff structures

## Operating Consequences

- Thesis quality matters more than point forecasts.
- Trade framing must respect changing state, not static labels.
- Risk management must account for timing and convexity, not just direction.
- System outputs must preserve ambiguity where the market is genuinely ambiguous.
